Update on Opportunity Youth in South King Co.

Analysis of Public Use Microdata Samples (PUMS) to Track Unemployed, Unenrolled 16-24 Year Olds

Using 2017 US Census PUMS data, we provide an update to the estimate of 'opportunity youth,' 16 to 24 year old people who are neither employed or enrolled in school, in South King Co. Washington. We compare these results to summarized 2014 data published here.

We find a lower total number of opportunity youth in the defined region and a lower proportion of opportunity youth from the 2014 data. In addition, we report that the proportion of opportunity youth without college experience has increased in comparison to those with some college experience.
